Tired of slow, buggy scanner apps from manufacturers that ruin the experience?
ScanBridge is a fast and reliable Android scanning app that connects to almost any network scanner using the AirScan / eSCL standard — no drivers or extra software required. One app, one clean experience, working seamlessly across devices.
ScanBridge makes using scanners fun again. What once felt old and clunky is now effortless, modern, and intuitive — exactly as it should be.
Features:
- Discover scanners supporting eSCL on your network
- Scan multiple pages and arrange them however you like
- Use your scanner to its full potential by adjusting settings such as by input source, resolution, duplex scan, scanning dimensions, and more
- Crop scans directly in-app
- Save your scans as PDF or images & directly share them with other apps
- Beautiful Material You design
- Simple and easy-to-use interface
- Access to prioritized support and feature requests
- 30-day refund guarantee if it does not work with your scanner (just reach out to our support at support@fireamp.eu)
ScanBridge is available for free on F-Droid.
By purchasing it here on the Play Store, you receive access to prioritized support and feature requests and help to support ongoing development so ScanBridge can continue to improve.
ScanBridge is open-source, with no ads or tracking. It is designed to be privacy-friendly and does not require any permissions, except the Internet permission to access the network scanner and to provide the support functions. No data/telemetry is collected.
