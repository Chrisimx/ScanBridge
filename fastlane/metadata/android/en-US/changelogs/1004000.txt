New Features:
- Automatic recovery of the most recent unfinished scan session when the app is launched, if present
- Automatic discovery of HTTPS scanners on the network
- Persistent storage of custom scanner connections for repeated use
- Option to disable HTTPS certificate verification in the settings (to easily connect to scanners with self-signed certs)
- Configurable maximum page count per PDF during export (adjustable in the app settings)
...and a couple of bug fixes
